MCore Law is a boutique international tax firm working across more than thirty-five
jurisdictions. Their old Wix site read like a flat list of services and pulled two
very different audiences —international and Mexican— in opposite directions.

I lead the **redesigned** and **rebuilt** the site on **Webflow**, handling **design** and **development** as a
single engagement. The new structure leads with a clear value proposition and routes
both audiences toward a free consultation, dressed in a serious palette of charcoal
and oxblood over warm off-white. The result is a presence that finally matches the
calibre of the firm's work—and is built to convert the right visitors into leads.
